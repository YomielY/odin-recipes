Spicy Tarka Lentil Daal

Description

This is probably the most cooked dish in India as it is a healthy source of vegetarian protein and is very satisfying. These buttery lentils are lifted with warming spices, zingy ginger, savoury garlic, and tangy tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Lentils 50% [Water, Lentils (Pigeon Pea, Mung Lentil)]
  • Onions
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Garlic
  • Turmeric

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tsp. oil/ghee in a sauce- pan until hot. Add the spices (add the chilli for optional extra heat). Take off the heat and cook for 15 seconds.
  2. Put the pan back on the heat and stir in the lentils (from the large pouch).
  3. Add in the tarka sauce (from the medium pouch) and 4-5 tbsp. (1/4 cup) of water, bring to a simmer and serve.
